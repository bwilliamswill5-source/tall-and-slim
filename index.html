Why Tall & Slim exists

If you’re tall and slim, you know the struggle: jeans that stop at your shins, sleeves that ride up, and “XL” just meaning wider, not longer. At 6'8", I’ve spent years trying to force regular clothes to work on a tall frame.

Tall & Slim was built to fix that. We’re curating brands and pieces that actually respect height — longer inseams, taller rises, extended sleeves and clean silhouettes that don’t swallow your frame.

  • ✅ Focused on tall & slim bodies only
  • ✅ Partnering with multiple streetwear brands
  • ✅ Simple filters: inseam, height range, fit
  • ✅ Inspo looks built on tall models
